Giant Frame is a small collective of musicians and friends who are infectiously curious about sound, form, and storytelling.

Luke is a stringed-things player and digital music producer. As in, he is digital, and produces music. His wireframe model is surprisingly lifelike.

Jesse is a CalArts-trained composer and percussionist. He'll talk your ear off about the roman empire, science fiction, and medieval french music.

Sometimes Logan, a literal genius, woodwind wunderkind, and past-life carnival barker, helps out too.

The three of us bubbled up out of the Great Salt Lake together, a briny singing hydra. Now we're based in Los Angeles CA and Buffalo NY.
